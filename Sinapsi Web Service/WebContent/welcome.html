What is Sinapsi


Sinapsi is a wide open platform that gives the users the ability to create and manage Macros

Macros

A Macro is something thats is composed by a Trigger and a list of Action. We can perform a lot of things, an example of macro can be "if i recive a message in my smartphone, show me a notification on my PC". As you can see, in this macro there is a condition ("if i recive") to run the macro, this condition in Sinapsi is called Trigger, in the Macro there are also a list of actions ("show me a notification"), we can perform a lot of actions in our devices connected to the Sinapsi account.

Triggers

The Trigger is the event that cause the execution of the Macro. Triggers are device-dependent. In our smartphone we can have triggers like: "if i recive a sms", "if i recive a phone call" and so on, but this triggers cannot be present in tablet device or pc device, because theren't the physical component to recive sms and phone call.

Actions

At this point we have the event that excute the Macro, we only need to define the actions to perfom at Macro execution, and in which device to execute the actions. Actions can be somthing like: "send email to ", "Show a notification", "Tweet on my account" and so on.

Brief story of Sinapsi

  • We saw the film "Her"

    beginning of 2015

    We saw the film "her" and we were amazed by the Artificial Intelligence of Samantha and her humanity, and we started dreaming of Being able to create something similar.

  • Computer Networks exam

    February 2015

    On the Occasion of exam of computer networks, we started to simplify our dreams regarding the super human AI for something doable. This was the birth of the Sinapsi project.

  • First commit

    April 2015

    After the birthday of Ayoub, we begin to write the initial part of the Sinapsi Engine code and Web Service code

  • Pre-Alpha 1.0

    July 2015

    We completed the first pre-alpha 1.0 of Sinapsi Engine and the Sinapsi Android client. The Desktop client is still incomplete, the Sinapsi Android client is still bugged, but we are on track, and we hope that Sinapsi have success.
